SINCE I TALK ABOUT HOW IMPORTANT IT IS FOR YOU TO SHARE YOUR STORY,

I WANTED TO SHARE MINE.

2017

THIS WAS THE YEAR OF NEW BEGINNINGS.

After I graduated college, I moved to Louisville, KY, and started my career as a writer for national fitness magazines. I was fired for “not being happy,” this led me to lose my apartment and my sense of self, and I slipped into a terrible depression. I started designing resumes out of coffee shops to afford gas, and that’s when I realized my deep love to design. I became obsessed with learning everything I could about design and would spend hours every night creating whatever I could.
